# Lesson 02: What Is ChatGPT?

## Skill Level

Beginner

## Time Needed

15 minutes

---

# Who This Helps

This lesson is for beginners who want to understand what ChatGPT and similar AI tools actually are.

---

# What You Will Learn

By the end of this lesson, you will:

* Understand what ChatGPT is
* Understand how conversational AI works
* Recognize practical uses
* Understand limitations
* Learn safe usage habits

---

# Why This Matters

ChatGPT is one of the most accessible AI tools available.

People use tools like ChatGPT for:

* Writing
* Learning
* Planning
* Brainstorming
* Customer communication
* Productivity
* Troubleshooting
* Personal organization

Understanding how it works helps you use it effectively.

---

# Learn

## ChatGPT is:

A conversational AI system designed to respond to prompts and questions using natural language.

---

# Think of ChatGPT Like:

* A digital assistant
* A writing helper
* A brainstorming partner
* A productivity support tool
* A research starting point

---

# ChatGPT Can Help With:

* Emails
* Reports
* Summaries
* Planning
* Writing
* Learning
* Organization
* Drafting ideas

---

# ChatGPT Cannot Guarantee:

* Perfect accuracy
* Legal certainty
* Medical certainty
* Financial certainty
* Human accountability
* Real-time truth without verification

---

# Important Rule

> ChatGPT predicts helpful responses, it does not “know” truth like a human expert.

---

# See

### Example:

```text id="p9fh2x"
Help me write a professional email requesting a repair timeline update.
```

### Result:

Useful draft in seconds.

# Common Beginner Mistakes

* Treating ChatGPT as always correct
* Being too vague
* Sharing sensitive information
* Skipping verification
* Expecting perfection

---

# Safety Check

Always:

* Verify important outputs
* Avoid private/confidential data
* Think critically
* Use judgment
