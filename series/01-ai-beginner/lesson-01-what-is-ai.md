# Learn

## AI means:

Artificial Intelligence

This refers to computer systems designed to perform tasks that normally require human-like thinking.

Examples include:

* Writing
* Summarizing
* Planning
* Pattern recognition
* Translation
* Answering questions
* Organizing information

---

# Simple Explanation

AI is not a human brain.

AI is a tool trained on large amounts of data that predicts useful outputs based on your input.

---

# Think of AI Like:

* A digital assistant
* A pattern recognition engine
* A productivity tool
* A brainstorming partner

---

# AI Is NOT:

* Magic
* Perfect
* Human
* Always truthful
* Fully self-aware
* A replacement for judgment

# Common Beginner Mistakes

* Thinking AI knows everything
* Trusting AI blindly
* Believing AI is human
* Overcomplicating its purpose
* Ignoring verification

---

# Safety Check

Always remember:

* AI can be wrong
* Verify important outputs
* Protect private information
* Use human judgment
