# Beginner Mistake

Many users:

* Overpay
* Use wrong tools
* Ignore privacy
* Assume all AI tools are equal

---

# Smart Tool Selection Questions

Before choosing a tool, ask:

* What task am I solving?
* Do I need privacy?
* Do I need speed?
* Do I need creativity?
* Do I need local control?
* Is this personal or professional?
