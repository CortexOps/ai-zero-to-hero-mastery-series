Using AI once is helpful.

# What Is an AI Workflow?

An AI workflow is:

## A repeatable process where AI helps complete tasks faster or better.

* No refinement
* Blind trust
* No verification
* Overcomplication

# Smart Workflow Habits

* Save useful prompts
* Build templates
* Reuse systems
* Verify outputs
* Improve over time
