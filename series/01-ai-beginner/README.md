# Series 01: Complete Beginner AI Foundations

## Welcome

This series is designed for people who are completely new to AI.

You do NOT need:

* Coding experience
* Technical skills
* Advanced knowledge
* Expensive software

You only need:

* Curiosity
* Willingness to practice
* Critical thinking
* A desire to improve

---

# Goal of This Series

By the end of Series 01, you will:

* Understand what AI is
* Understand what ChatGPT and similar tools are
* Learn what prompts are
* Understand why AI can make mistakes
* Learn safe AI habits
* Complete practical beginner exercises
* Build real confidence
