# Lesson 03: What Is a Prompt?

## Skill Level

Beginner

## Time Needed

15–20 minutes

---

# Who This Helps

This lesson is for beginners who want to understand how to communicate effectively with AI.

---

# What You Will Learn

By the end of this lesson, you will:

* Understand what a prompt is
* Learn why prompts matter
* Recognize weak vs strong prompts
* Improve AI results
* Build better communication habits

---

# Why This Matters

AI quality depends heavily on input quality.

Simply put:

> Better prompts = Better results

Learning prompts is one of the fastest ways to improve your AI success.

---

# Learn

## A prompt is:

The instruction, question, or request you give to AI.

---

# Simple Example

### Weak Prompt:

```text id="6ccgk8"
Write email
```

### Better Prompt:

```text id="zyd4v9"
Write a short professional email to my supervisor explaining that a replacement part is needed before the plumbing repair can be completed.
```

---

# Why Better Prompts Work

Better prompts provide:

* Goal
* Context
* Role
* Desired tone
* Desired format

---

# Basic Prompt Formula

## Simple:

```text id="7p8n2w"
Help me with [specific task].
```

---

# Stronger Formula:

```text id="7w2jfn"
Act as [role].
Help me [goal].
Context: [details].
Format: [desired output].
```

---

# Real-World Examples

## Trades:

```text id="azn93g"
Write a customer-friendly plumbing repair summary.
```

## Office:

```text id="93gvzd"
Draft a respectful meeting follow-up email.
```

## Job Seeker:

```text id="0p7vsk"
Improve this resume bullet for professionalism.
```

## IT:

```text id="7u2wgh"
Create a troubleshooting checklist for login failures.
```

---

# See

### Vague Prompt:

```text id="v8a7nc"
Help me
```

### Useful Prompt:

```text id="y0pj7n"
Act as an office assistant.
Help me write a professional email requesting an update on delayed equipment repairs.
Keep it concise.
```

---

# Do

Practice:

Choose one real task and ask AI using this structure:

```text id="7b4mcu"
Act as [role].
Help me [goal].
Context: [real details].
Format: [desired style].
```

---

# Reflection

Ask:

* Was the answer better?
* Did context improve results?
* What details mattered most?
* How can I improve further?

---

# Common Beginner Mistakes

* Too vague
* No context
* No format
* No role
* Overcomplicating unnecessarily
* Blind trust

---

# Safety Check

Remember:

* Prompts influence output quality
* AI can still be wrong
* Verify important answers
* Never share sensitive data carelessly

---

# Real-World Challenge

Take one weak prompt you have used before and improve it.

---

# Confidence Builder

If you improve your prompts:

> You immediately become a stronger AI user.

---

# Key Takeaway

> Prompts are how humans guide AI effectively.
