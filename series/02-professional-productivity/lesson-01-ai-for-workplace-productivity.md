# Important Rule

> AI should assist, not replace critical review.

---

# Workplace Safety

Never input:

* Confidential HR data
* Sensitive legal records
* Student records
* Medical records
* Passwords
* Financial account details

---

# Professional Safe Use

Use AI for:

* Structure
* Drafting
* Brainstorming
* Productivity

Not for:

* Final legal decisions
* Sensitive compliance
* Confidential disclosures

---

# Prompting Tips for Professionals

## Better:

* Be clear
* Include role
* Include audience
* Include tone
* Include desired outcome
