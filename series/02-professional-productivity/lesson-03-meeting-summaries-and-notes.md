# Ideal Meeting Summary Structure

---

## Executive Summary

* Purpose
* Major outcomes

---

## Key Decisions

* Approved changes
* Strategic direction
* Blockers resolved

---

## Action Items

* Task
* Owner
* Deadline

---

## Risks / Concerns

* Issues
* Dependencies
* Escalations

---

## Next Steps

* Upcoming milestones
* Follow-up meetings
