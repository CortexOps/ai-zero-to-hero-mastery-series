# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in the AI Zero to Hero Mastery Series community a respectful, safe, inclusive, and empowering experience for everyone.

This project exists to expand free AI literacy and practical education across all professions, backgrounds, and skill levels.

---

# Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Respectful communication
* Constructive feedback
* Beginner-friendly support
* Ethical AI advocacy
* Professional collaboration
* Inclusive teaching
* Curiosity and continuous learning
* Practical empowerment

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment
* Discrimination
* Hate speech
* Personal attacks
* Gatekeeping
* Deliberate misinformation
* Unsafe AI recommendations
* Malicious contributions
* Intimidation
* Trolling

---

# Educational Integrity

Contributors should prioritize:

* Accuracy
* Practicality
* Accessibility
* Safety
* Transparency
* Ethical responsibility

---

# Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

* Reviewing contributions
* Maintaining quality standards
* Enforcing respectful collaboration
* Removing harmful or unsafe material
* Protecting community integrity

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* Repository discussions
* Pull requests
* Issues
* Community spaces
* Educational contributions
* Public representation of the project

---

# Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report concerns to project maintainers.

---

# Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

# Mission Alignment

This project is built to empower people through ethical, practical AI education.

Community behavior should always reflect that mission.

---

# Final Standard

Be respectful.
Be practical.
Be ethical.
Help people grow.
