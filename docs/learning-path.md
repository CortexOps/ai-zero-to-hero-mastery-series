# AI Zero to Hero Mastery Series Learning Path

Welcome to the complete AI learning journey.

This roadmap is designed to take learners from complete beginner to confident AI user, advanced builder, and strategic leader.

---

# Stage 1: Orientation

## Series 00

### Goal:

Understand:

* What this project is
* How to use it
* AI basics
* Learning expectations
* Safety mindset

### Outcome:

The learner completes their first useful AI task.

# Stage 2: Beginner Foundations

## Series 01

### Goal:

* What ChatGPT is
* Prompt basics
* Common mistakes
* AI limitations
* Safety fundamentals

### Outcome:

The learner becomes a safe, practical beginner.
