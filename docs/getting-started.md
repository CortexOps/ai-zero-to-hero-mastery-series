# Getting Started with AI Zero to Hero Mastery Series

Welcome.

This guide is designed to help anyone, regardless of technical skill, begin learning practical AI safely and effectively.

---

# Who This Guide Is For

This project is built for:

* Complete beginners
* Workers
* Tradespeople
* Office professionals
* Educators
* IT staff
* Business owners
* Job seekers
* Leaders
* Lifelong learners

# What You Need

You do NOT need:

* Coding skills
* Advanced technical knowledge
* Expensive software
* High-end hardware

You DO need:

* Curiosity
* Willingness to practice
* Critical thinking
* Ethical responsibility

# Beginner Rules

## Always:

* Verify AI outputs
* Protect private data
* Use AI ethically
* Ask questions
* Practice regularly

## Never:

* Assume AI is always correct
* Share sensitive personal information
* Use AI irresponsibly
* Skip verification
