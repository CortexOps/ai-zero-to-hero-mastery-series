# AI Zero to Hero Mastery Series

A free, open-source AI training guide that helps anyone go from complete beginner to confident AI power user.

This project is built for everyday people, workers, students, tradespeople, office staff, educators, small business owners, IT professionals, and future leaders.

You do not need a technical background.
You do not need coding experience.
You do not need expensive tools.

You only need curiosity and the willingness to practice.

## Mission

To provide world-class, practical AI education for anyone, regardless of career path, technical skill, or background.

## Who This Is For

- Complete beginners
- Maintenance workers
- Plumbers, electricians, and tradespeople
- Office workers
- Teachers and school staff
- IT professionals
- Job seekers
- Small business owners
- Leaders and managers
- Anyone who wants to learn AI for free

## What You Will Learn

- What AI is
- How to use AI safely
- Prompt engineering
- Workplace AI
- Business AI
- Automation
- Local AI
- Leadership with AI
